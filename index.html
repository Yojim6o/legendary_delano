Loki

This Week's Mastermind

  • Attack Power: 10 (High)
  • Always Leads the Enemies of Asgard
  • Master Strike: Player must reveal a strength hero or else gain a wound.

His Scheme: Midtown Bank Robbery

Loki is quickly amassing a large force of villains through his mind-control, and testing their mettle by directing their power toward infiltrating the midtown bank, the largest financial institution in the world. Obviously his powers have no need for world currency, but Loki's ultimate plans are never easy to surmise. SHIELD has been able to gather important pieces of information for us before the battle begins.

  • As of right now, we estimate 1200 innocent bystanders have been captured, but are still within the city and able to be rescued.
  • Loki is awarding additional strength to villains with bystanders in their clutches.
  • The villains have plans to capture additional bystanders at the bank.
  • SHIELD will declare this mission a failure if villains escape the city with eight or more bystander groups.

Enemies of Asgard

The Enemies of Asgard are a variety of powerful villains that are best fought with ranged heroes.

Skrulls

The Skrulls excel at capturing heroes from HQ and converting their recruitment cost to attack power. These heroes can be rescued when the Skrull is defeated.

Masters of Evil

The Masters of Evil are very strong villains that, if attacked strategicly, provide bonuses to players.

HYDRA

The HYDRA are a force of unique villains that can quickly build up in numbers.

Doombot Legion

Doombot Legion is an army of Dr. Doom clones. When they are attacked, you must discard one of two cards on the top of your deck.

Savage Land Mutates

If you can survive these angry savages, draw an extra card at the end of your turn.

Captain America

CAPTAIN AMERICA grants bonuses for card type variety
ULTIMATE: bonus attack power for each other Avenger you played in a single turn

Nick Fury

NICK FURY provides bonus attack power for tech and avenger type cards
ULTIMATE: kill a villain or hit the mastermind if they have less attack power than the number of dead avengers

Cyclops

CYCLOPS has strong attack power requiring discards. Some abilties cancel the discard effect
ULTIMATE: bonus attack power for each other X-Man you played this turn

Iron Man

IRON MAN has bonus card drawing and attack bonus abilities
ULTIMATE: draw two cards or 4 cards if you have a tech hero

Black Widow

BLACK WIDOW utilizes bystanders for bonuses and card drawing
ULTIMATE: kill a villain or hit the mastermind if they captured a bystander

Wolverine

WOLVERINE has bonus card drawing and can discard wounds for bonus attack power
ULTIMATE: draw three cards. Gain attack power for each extra card you've drawn if you have an instinct hero

Hulk

HULK accumulates significant attack power bonuses using wounds and strength heroes. Team members suffer collateral damange wounds
ULTIMATE: 5 attack plus another 5 if a strength hero is played

Hawkeye

HAWKEYE creates card drawing bonuses for player and team members, as well as attack bonuses for Avenger heroes
ULTIMATE: rescue 3 bystanders if you kill a villain or hit the mastermind

Storm

STORM can move villains to different city spaces, and nerf villains depending on their location
ULTIMATE: nerf the villain on the bridge. Also nerf the mastermind if you have a ranged card

Thor

THOR is good for building recruitment points and rewarding those points with attack bonuses
ULTIMATE: convert all recruitment points to attack power that turn

Thor

SPIDERMAN is a good early game hero with low cost recruitment and bonus card drawing abilities
ULTIMATE: reveal 3 top cards of your deck. If their cost is low, put them in your hand

Rogue

ROGUE can copy abilities from other cards, including ultimates
ULTIMATE: teammates discard one card from their decks. Player plays all of those cards

Emma Frost

EMMA FROST is an anti-hero with card drawing bonuses and attack bonuses, at the cost of summoning villains
ULTIMATE: get additional recruitment points if you defeat a villain or hit the mastermind that turn

Deadpool

DEADPOOL is an anti-hero with an odd choice of abilities that may help or hurt the team
ULTIMATE: take a wound. Each player hands another player a card in their hand

Gambit

GAMBIT has many abilities for hand/deck interchange for the player and their teammates
ULTIMATE: reveal the top card in your deck, gain attack power equal to its cost
